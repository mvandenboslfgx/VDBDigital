# Webshop – business rules

## Customer-facing content only

The webshop must **never** show:

- Supplier information
- Alibaba or other marketplace names
- Manufacturer name
- Wholesale source
- Purchase price
- Dropshipping source
- Supplier links

All supplier and sourcing information stays **private and internal**. Only customer-facing product information (name, price, description, specs, images, availability, delivery) may be visible on the site.
