VDB DIGITAL PROJECT SETUP

0. Environment & database

- Copy .env.example to .env and fill in all values (DATABASE_URL, Supabase, Stripe, OpenAI, etc.)
- Run: npm install && npx prisma migrate deploy && npx prisma db seed
- For Google OAuth: enable Google in Supabase Dashboard → Auth → Providers; add redirect URLs (see .env.example)
- Product images: add tvbox1.jpg, tvbox2.jpg, tvbox3.jpg, tvbox4.jpg to public/products/ (or use placeholder)

1. .gitignore regels toevoegen

Open .gitignore en zorg dat dit erin staat:

.env
node_modules
.next

4. Founder Story

Matthijs van den Bos has a background in sales and commercial environments including telecom and multiple industries.

Through experience building websites, running dropshipping projects and working with online systems he discovered that digital tools are often fragmented and complicated.

Businesses often need multiple platforms for websites, marketing, analytics and automation.

VDB Digital was created to simplify digital growth and give entrepreneurs one place where they can manage everything.
